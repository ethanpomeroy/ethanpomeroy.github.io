About

What I'm studying and why it matters

I study Mathematics at the University of Birmingham with a concentration in Analysis. My current interests include real & complex analysis (rigour & proof), functional analysis (operator viewpoints), Fourier analysis, Differential Equations, and Linear Algebra. I enjoy writing clean LaTeX notes and building small tools that visualize or verify results.
